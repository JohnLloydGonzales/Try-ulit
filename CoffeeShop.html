Kremstick Coffee Menu

Americano

(Hot or Iced)

Combines freshly pulled shots of espresso with hot water to achieve the size of a standard cup of brewed coffee.

Cappucino

(Hot or Iced)

The perfect balance of espresso, steamed milk and foam.

Vanilla Latte

(Iced)

Made with full-bodied espresso, creamy steamed milk and classic vanilla syrup.

Macchiato

(Hot or Iced)

A single shot of espresso with a splash of textured milk.

Irish Coffee

(Hot or Cold)

A caffeinated alcoholic drink consisting of Irish whiskey, hot coffee and sugar, which has been stirred and topped with cream (sometimes cream liqueur).

Cafe Latte

(Iced)

A milk coffee that is a made up of one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk and a final, thin layer of frothed milk on top.

Espresso

(Hot or Iced)

A concentrated form of coffee brewed with high pressure, hot water and finely ground coffee beans.

Coffee Mocha

(Iced)

A shot of espresso is combined with a chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream.

Spanish Latte

(Iced)

Made with one part espresso, one part milk, and one part condensed milk.

Classic Brew

(Hot)

Made by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans and allowing the mixture to steep for several minutes, allowing the water to extract the flavour and aroma of the coffee beans.

Vietnamese Latte

(Iced)

Its distinct flavor comes from the use of Robusta beans and sweetened condensed milk, which contribute to its thicker texture and rich taste.

Coffee Caramel

(Hot or Iced)

Combines the sweet taste of caramel with the rich, aromatic flavours of coffee beans.

Arabica

(Hot)

Slightly sweet flavour accompanied by hints of chocolate, caramel and nuts.

Caramel Coffee Jelly

(Iced)

An amazing combination of three delectable layers: creative coffee jelly, a rich caramel Frappuccino with milk, coffee, and ice, and a fluffy mound of whipped cream drizzled with caramel.

Mocha Caramel Coffee

(Hot and Iced)

A shot of mocha is combined with a Caramel syrup, followed by cream.

Turkish Latte

(Cold)

Bold and condensed flavour which can be described as bittersweet.

Dalgona Coffee (Special)

(Iced)

A whipped, frothy iced coffee drink made with instant coffee, sugar, water, and milk.

Flat White

(Hot)

An espresso-based coffee drink accompanied with steamed milk and microfoam.

Chai Latte

(Iced)

A blend of bold black tea, a symphony of spices like cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, and cloves, and the creamy richness of milk.

Brown Sugar Iced Latte

(Iced)

Brown sugar has a nice toasty flavor to it.
